About Inam Services

Founded in 2008, Inam Services has been at the forefront of providing specialized security and fire safety solutions for the holy sites in Makkah. Our team of experts combines cutting-edge technology with deep understanding of the unique requirements of these sacred spaces.

We are honored to serve the Grand Mosque and surrounding areas, ensuring the safety and security of millions of pilgrims who visit each year. Our commitment to excellence and innovation has made us the trusted partner for authorities managing the holy sites.
